Privacy Policy for PDF to Excel,

Last updated: 20-03-2026

This Privacy Policy explains how PDF to Excel (“we”, “our”, or “us”) handles information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect
1.1 Files you choose to process

When you upload a PDF or image to our app, we process that file to extract content and convert it into an Excel file. The uploaded file is sent through our processing pipeline, which uses the Gemini API and our Python-based conversion service to generate the output.

1.2 Device information

We collect limited device information to help us identify and fix device-specific issues and respond to user feedback. This may include:

Device model

Android version / operating system version

App version

Crash logs

Error logs

Diagnostic information related to app performance

1.3 No account information

Our app does not require login or account creation. We do not collect usernames, passwords, or authentication credentials because the app does not have an account system.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect only for the following purposes:

To analyze uploaded PDFs or images

To convert documents into Excel files

To improve conversion accuracy

To troubleshoot bugs and device-specific issues

To respond to support requests and user feedback

To maintain and improve app performance

3. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share or transmit limited data only as needed to provide the app’s core service, including:

Google Gemini API for file analysis

Backend processing services used to convert the extracted data into Excel format

Hosting / infrastructure providers that help us operate the app

We only use such services to process and support the app’s functionality.

4. Data Retention

We keep information only for as long as necessary to:

Provide the conversion service

Troubleshoot problems

Improve app performance

Meet legal or security obligations

Uploaded files and processing data are not intended to be stored longer than necessary for the conversion process, unless retention is required for support, debugging, security, or legal reasons.

5. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that such information has been collected, we will take steps to delete it.

7. Your Choices

Because our app does not use accounts or login, there is no account data to manage or delete inside the app.
